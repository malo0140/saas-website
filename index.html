About PocketMoney

Be your own personal accountant! Keep track of the money you earn and spend monthly on bills and personal wants and needs. Create a monthly budget to keep you on track with your money! Get notices to pay your bills on time.

Alerts

Can’t remember all those due dates for your bills? Set alerts to notify the date, days or a week before they are due to pay your bills on time. Need to stick to your budget? Create notifications when you have gone over your set budget to stop spending.

Secure

Don’t want people to look at your app if you loose your phone? Password protect your app and have access to if from any computer with your email and password. Lock or change your password from anywhere with simple and safe access.

Store Recepts

Take photos of your receipts to keep the clutter of paper out of your house! Obtain organization fast by utilizing different categories to place your files into, minimize stress and maximize productivity. Our number recognition will do all the work.

Budget

Create a budget that’s right for you or your business! Use graphs to compare your spending month to month, keeping your spending on track and money in your pocket.

Connect

Connect to your bank and pay your bills directly from the app. When notifyed for a bill just click Pay Now! This app will do all the hard work while you stress less about finances.

Support

Having trouble with the website or app? We can assist you in the most conveinent way to you. We answer by Phone, live chat, e-mail and social media. 24/7 we answer!
